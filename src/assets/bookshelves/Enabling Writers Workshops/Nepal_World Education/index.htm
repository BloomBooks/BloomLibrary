This bookshelf comprises the collection of 1432 decodable and tiered reader’s titles in 6 local languages and one national language in Nepal. All the titles of this bookshelf are aligned with early grade curriculum recommended by the Ministry of Education and Science and Technology – Government of Nepal. Early grade teachers, students, local linguists, Education activists, parents and teacher trainers have volunteered to develop these titles. The major objective of these decodable and tiered titles are to support Early Grade Children of Nepal to develop reading competency. Moreover, the values of diverse culture, language, and dialect existing in the country are taught and reading in 7 languages is promoted.
